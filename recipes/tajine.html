Tajine recipe

Description

A Tajine or tagine Arabic is a Moroccan dish, named after the earthenware pot in which it is cooked. It is also called maraq or marqa.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • 3 carrots , cut into chunks
  • 3 large parsnips , cut into chunks
  • 3 red onions , cut into chunks
  • 2large potatoes , cut into chunks
  • 4 leeks , ends trimmed and cut into chunks
  • 2 dried prunes , dates or figs
  • 2 sprigs mint , leaves only, finely chopped

Steps

  1. put everything
  2. cook
  3. enjoy
